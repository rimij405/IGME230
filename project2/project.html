Instructions.

Flag down all the mines! You have the same amount of flags as there are mines!

Right-click squares to place "!" flags to diffuse mines on reveal.

Right-click, again, to place "?" flags to keep track of spaces you're unsure of - be warned, this won't diffuse the mine if you reveal one!

Right-click, a third time to remove any existing flags.

Revealing a mine that isn't being diffused will blow up the game!

When clicking on a space, you'll unlock some neighboring cells, as long as they aren't mines!

You'll receive points for every square that you reveal and every mine you successfully diffuse!

Diffused mines are marked by a "©" symbol.
