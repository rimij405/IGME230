Olympia 2608!

A 2D hack n' slash, with a neon, futuristic look, inspired by the cybernetic Olympic games, in the year 2608.

Game Treatment

In order to showcase the power of PIXI.js, project 3 has evolved from a simple typewriting game (a la Mavis Beacon), now will challenge the developer to create a more visually satisfying interactive experience.

High Concept

History repeats itself; like the Roman collesiums of old, cybernetic gladiators will duke it out in the post-postmodern neon world games series: Olympia Rising, in the year of 2608. And what better way than to solve it with Rock Paper and Scissors.

Genre

A point-n-click with an emphasis on chance.

Platform

Browser only.

Story

Everyone competes; andn somethings can only be solved when you say, "Shoot".

Aesthetic

There is an emphasis on making it very clear and direct: this is a very, simple game involving PIXI.js.

Gameplay

The following section covers the gameplay portion of the document.

Mechanics

Mechancis are simply this. Rock beats Scissors. Scissors beats Paper. And Paper bests Rock.

Controls

Player Learning

Players play against chance. It's a bit like gambling.
